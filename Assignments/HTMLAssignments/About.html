Alia Bhatt

Name: Alia Bhatt
Born: 15 March 1993, Mumbai, India
Profession: Actress, Producer
Industry: Bollywood
Debut Movie: Student of the Year (2012)


Education

Alia Bhatt completed her schooling from Jamnabai Narsee School, Mumbai.
She developed an interest in acting from a young age.
Her dedication and talent made her one of the leading actresses in India.


Achievements

Alia Bhatt has won multiple Filmfare Awards for her performances.
She is known for movies like Highway, Raazi, Dear Zindagi and Gangubai Kathiawadi.
She is admired for her versatile acting skills.
