Momos

Momos are a type of steamed filled dumpling in Tibetan, Nepali, and
Indian cuisine. They are made with a plain flour-based dough and
filled with savory stuffing, often including vegetables. Momos
have become popular street food in India

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular dish from the northern regions
of India, consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served
with deep-fried bread (bhature). This dish is known for its
unique blend of flavors and textures, making it a beloved
street food and a staple in many households.
